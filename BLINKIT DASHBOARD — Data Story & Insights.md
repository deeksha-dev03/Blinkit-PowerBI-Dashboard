**Business Context**
Blinkit functions as a fast-paced commerce platform which generates revenue and maintains customer relationships through its ability to deliver rapid
service and dependable operations while understanding its customer base.
This dashboard analyzes sales performance, customer behavior, and delivery efficiency to help stakeholders take data-driven decisions.

## **Key Insights**
**Insight 1: Sales Growth & Seasonality (Sales Trend Chart)**
What the data shows
1.Sales follow a monthly trend with visible peaks
2.Certain months contribute disproportionately to total revenue

Insight : Sales are seasonal, indicating demand spikes during specific months (likely festivals or offers).

Business Impact
Inventory & delivery resources should be scaled up during peak months
Marketing campaigns can be aligned with high-conversion periods


**Insight 2: Customer Segments Drive Revenue (Customer Segment Chart)**
What the data shows
1.Premium customers contribute the highest sales
2.Regular customers drive volume but lower AOV
3.Inactive customers have minimal contribution

Insight : A small premium segment generates a large share of revenue.

Business Impact
Retention programs should prioritize premium users
Personalized offers can increase AOV for regular customers


**Insight 3: Delivery Performance Affects Customer Trust (Donut / KPI)**
What the data shows
1.High % of on-time deliveries
2.A small but critical % of delayed orders

Insight : Even minor delivery delays can impact customer satisfaction in quick commerce.

Business Impact
Optimize delivery partner allocation
Focus on reducing delays in high-order areas

 
**Insight 4: Payment Preferences (Donut / Bar Chart)**
What the data shows
1.Digital payment methods dominate
2.COD usage is significantly lower

Insight : Customers prefer fast, frictionless digital payments.

Business Impact
Promote prepaid orders to reduce delivery friction
Incentivize UPI usage for faster checkout


**Insight 5: Area / Category Performance (Bar Chart)**
What the data shows
1.Top areas or categories contribute most of sales
2.Long tail of low-performing regions/categories

Insight : Revenue is concentrated in a few high-performing locations/categories.

Business Impact
Expand operations in high-growth zones
Re-evaluate underperforming areas
